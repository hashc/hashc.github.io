Haochuan is the Assistant Professor and Associate Researcher of School of Journalism and Communication at Nanjing Normal University. His research lies in the rising fields of the Science of Science& Innovation and Computational Social Science. He uses and develops leading analytics tools (such as large-scale data analysis, complex network analysis, deep learning) to understand the formation of scientific progress — from individual behavior to collective progress.
